# Spicy Salmon (Sushi Grade)

A high-protein, low-fat preparation of spicy salmon for sushi, bowls, or snacks.

**Protein:** Salmon | **Dairy-Free:** Yes | **Prep Time:** 20 mins

## Tags
- **Culinary Structure:** Quick / Under 20 Mins
- **Macro Profile:** Lean Protein Focus, Healthy Fats / Recovery
- **Primary Stores:** Sprouts

## Normalized Macros
- **Calories:** 471.0
- **Protein:** 49.5g
- **Carbs:** 28.0g
- **Fat:** 3.0g

## Ingredients
- 226g sushi grade salmon
- 30g light mayo
- 10g sriracha
- Spring onions
- 4g sesame oil
- Sesame seeds (optional)

## Steps
1. Dice the sushi-grade salmon into small cubes.
2. In a bowl, combine salmon with light mayo, sriracha, chopped spring onions, and sesame oil.
3. Mix well and top with sesame seeds if desired.
4. Serve as is, in sushi, or over rice (note: macros provided are without rice).
