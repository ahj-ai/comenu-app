# Numbing Chicken and Cucumber Salad

A spicy and numbing Sichuan-inspired chicken salad from the 50/30 Low Cal series.

**Macros:** Over 50g protein, low calorie

## Ingredients
- Chicken breast
- Cucumber
- Soy sauce
- Black vinegar
- Chili oil
- Garlic
- Sichuan peppercorns
- Cilantro
- Sesame seeds
- 450g Chicken Breast
- 20g Ginger
- 2 Spring Onions (for poaching)
- 1 tsp Msg
- 1 tbsp Salt
- 1-2 Whole Cucumbers
- 3 Spring Onions (for serving)
- 4 Garlic Cloves, Minced (for sauce)
- 1 tsp Sichuan Peppercorns
- 1 tbsp Sugar
- 2 tsp Sesame Oil
- 2 tbsp Chinese Black Vinegar
- 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
- 1 tbsp Dark Soy
- 2 tbsp Chilli Oil
- 6 tbsp Poaching Liquid
- Coriander
- 200g Cooked Short grain Rice

## Steps
1. Poach and shred the chicken breast.
2. Slice the cucumbers.
3. Whisk together soy sauce, black vinegar, chili oil, garlic, and Sichuan peppercorns.
4. Toss chicken and cucumbers in the sauce.
5. Garnish with cilantro and sesame seeds.
6. Poach chicken: add ginger, spring onions, msg, and salt to a pan with water. Bring to a simmer, add chicken, simmer for 3 minutes, then cover and turn off heat for 10 minutes. Remove and slice or shred.
7. Mix all sauce ingredients together, including the poaching liquid, and set aside to chill.
8. Slice cucumber on an angle into thick strips.
9. Scatter cucumbers on a platter, top with sliced chicken, and pour over the sauce. Finish with coriander, spring onions, and sesame seeds. Serve with warm rice.
