# Air Fried Spicy McChicken

A healthier, high-protein, air-fried version of the classic McChicken sandwich.

**Protein:** Chicken | **Dairy-Free:** Yes | **Prep Time:** 30 mins

## Tags
- **Culinary Structure:** Bowl / Multi-Component
- **Macro Profile:** Lean Protein Focus
- **Primary Stores:** Target, Safeway

## Normalized Macros
- **Calories:** 196.0
- **Protein:** 32.0g
- **Carbs:** N/A
- **Fat:** N/A

## Ingredients
- 80g to 600g chicken breast or tenderloin
- Panko crumbs
- Hot Sauce/Buffalo Sauce
- Paprika
- Garlic Powder
- Onion Powder
- Salt
- Black Pepper
- Low calorie bun (e.g., Culture Keto Buns)
- Romaine Lettuce
- Light Mayo (e.g., Miracle Whip light mayo)

## Steps
1. Mince the chicken if using breast or tenderloin.
2. Combine chicken with paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper.
3. Form into patties (approximately 105g each for single servings).
4. Coat each patty in panko crumbs.
5. Air fry the patties until cooked through (internal 165F).
6. Assemble the sandwich with a low-calorie bun, light mayo, the spicy chicken patty, and romaine lettuce.
