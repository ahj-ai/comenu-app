# High Protein Mac and Cheese

A healthy, high-protein version of comfort food mac and cheese, ready in under 10 minutes.

**Macros:** Type: High protein, low calorie, Time: <10 mins

## Ingredients
- Pasta
- High-protein cheese sauce

## Steps
1. Cook pasta until tender.
2. Combine with prepared high-protein, low-calorie cheese sauce.
3. Serve warm.
