# Creamy Cajun Salmon Pasta

Creamy Cajun salmon pasta made with tender seared salmon bites tossed in a rich tomato-cream sauce, Cajun spices, garlic, and a splash of lemon for balance.

**Protein:** Salmon | **Dairy-Free:** No | **Prep Time:** 30 mins

## Ingredients
- Salmon (1 lb, cubed)
- Cajun seasoning
- onion powder
- garlic salt
- paprika
- olive oil
- Onion
- garlic
- tomato paste
- heavy cream
- Cajun seasoning
- paprika
- salt
- pepper
- lemon juice
- Pasta + reserved pasta water
- Parsley

## Steps
1. Season salmon with Cajun seasoning, onion powder, garlic salt, paprika + olive oil. Sear until cooked, set aside.
2. In same pan, sauté onion + garlic. Stir in tomato paste and cook down.
3. Add heavy cream + seasonings + lemon juice.
4. Loosen sauce with pasta water until silky.
5. Toss in pasta, then fold in salmon.
6. Finish with parsley.
