# Protein Chick-fil-A Sauce

A macro-friendly, high-protein version of the famous Chick-Fil-A sauce using Greek yogurt.

**Macros:** 26g protein per jar, low calorie

## Ingredients
- Non-fat Greek yogurt
- Mustard
- Honey
- BBQ sauce
- Lemon juice
- Paprika
- 1 1/4 cups (280g) fat-free Greek yogurt
- 7 tbsp (105g) yellow mustard
- 3 tbsp (45g) light mayo
- 3 tbsp (45g) sugar-free BBQ sauce
- 1/2 tbsp (10g) honey
- 1.5 tsp (7.5g) apple cider vinegar
- 1.5 tsp (4.5g) onion powder
- 4 tsp (4g) stevia
- Salt to taste
- Fat-free Fairlife milk (optional, to thin)

## Steps
1. Combine yogurt, mustard, honey, BBQ sauce, lemon juice, and paprika in a jar.
2. Whisk until completely smooth.
3. Mix all ingredients in a jar until well combined.
4. Add milk if a thinner consistency is desired.
5. Enjoy as a dip or sandwich spread.
