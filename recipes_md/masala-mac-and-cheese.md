# Masala Mac and Cheese

Creamy, cozy fusion mac and cheese packed with bold Indian flavors.

**Macros:** Not specified

## Ingredients
- Pasta
- Green chili
- Onions
- Ginger
- Garlic
- Bell peppers
- Spices
- Tomato paste
- Butter
- Flour
- Milk
- Cream
- Cheese
- Breadcrumbs

## Steps
1. Saute green chili, onions, ginger, garlic, and bell peppers.
2. Add spices and tomato paste for the base.
3. Prepare a roux with butter, flour, milk, and cream.
4. Stir in cheese until smooth, then add cooked pasta.
5. Top with more cheese and breadcrumbs.
6. Bake until golden brown.
