# Animal Style In N Out Pasta

A pasta dish inspired by In N Out's animal style flavors.

**Protein:** Beef | **Dairy-Free:** No | **Prep Time:** 20 mins

## Tags
- **Culinary Structure:** Quick / Under 20 Mins
- **Macro Profile:** Lean Protein Focus
- **Primary Stores:** Safeway

- **Fat:** N/A

## Ingredients
- 1 box pasta
- 900g 96/4 lean ground beef
- Parsley
- 2-3 onion
- 80g cheese sauce
- 8g neutral oil for onions
- 300g cottage cheese (for sauce)
- 50g pasta water (for sauce)
- 90g ketchup (for sauce)
- 50g sweet relish (for sauce)

## Steps
1. Cook pasta and reserve water.
2. Prepare animal sauce by blending cottage cheese, pasta water, ketchup, and relish.
3. Cook ground beef and sauté onions in oil.
4. Combine pasta, beef, onions, cheese sauce, and animal sauce. Finish with parsley.
