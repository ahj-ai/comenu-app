# Slow Cooker Honey Chipotle Chicken Burritos

High protein, meal-prep friendly burritos made with tender shredded chicken in a creamy chipotle sauce.

**Protein:** Chicken | **Dairy-Free:** No | **Prep Time:** 480 mins

## Tags
- **Culinary Structure:** Meal Prep / Batch, Bowl / Multi-Component
- **Macro Profile:** Lean Protein Focus, Healthy Fats / Recovery
- **Primary Stores:** Safeway

## Normalized Macros
- **Calories:** 493.0
- **Protein:** 57.5g
- **Carbs:** 46.0g
- **Fat:** 11.5g

## Ingredients
- 60oz Chicken Breast (1700g)
- 2 tbsps Garlic Salt
- 1 tbsp Onion Powder
- 1 Can Chipotle Peppers (7oz)
- 3 tbsps Honey
- 1 Cup Cottage Cheese (0% Fat)
- 1 Cup Tomato Sauce
- 4 Garlic Cloves
- 0.5 Cup Fat Free Milk
- 1 Cup Dry Rice (cooked)
- 5 Cups Fat Free Mozzarella
- 10 Low Calorie Tortillas (e.g., Extreme Wellness wraps)
- Optional: 1/4 avocado, fat reduced Mexican cheese

## Steps
1. Add chicken breast, garlic salt, onion powder, chipotle peppers, and honey to a crockpot. Cook until chicken is tender enough to shred.
2. Prepare the sauce by blending cottage cheese, tomato sauce, garlic, and milk.
3. Cook the rice separately.
4. Shred the cooked chicken and mix with the prepared sauce and cooked rice.
5. Assemble the burritos by adding the chicken mixture and fat-free mozzarella to the tortillas.
6. Optionally add avocado or extra cheese before rolling.
