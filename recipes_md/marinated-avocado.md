# Marinated Avocado

A perfect side-dish recipe for meal-prep featuring marinated avocado slices.

**Protein:** None | **Dairy-Free:** Yes | **Prep Time:** 30 mins

## Ingredients
- Avocado
- 100ml of soy sauce
- 100ml of water
- 2tbsp of honey
- 1tbsp of sesame oil
- Green onions
- 1 clove of minced or grated garlic
- 1tbsp of chili flakes or powder
- 1tbsp of sesame seeds
- Minced onion (optional)

## Steps
1. Slice avocado.
2. In a container, add all marinade ingredients and mix well.
3. Put sliced avocado into marinated sauce.
4. Let it rest in the fridge for about 2-3 hours.
5. Enjoy with rice.
