# Thai Basil Beef Inspired Bowl

A quick, easy, and flavorful beef bowl perfect for weeknight dinners or meal prep.

**Macros:** Not specified

## Ingredients
- Peppers
- Onions
- Lean ground beef
- Garlic
- Ginger
- Green onion
- Basil
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Soy sauce
- Fish sauce
- Honey
- Brown sugar

## Steps
1. Sauté peppers and onions in a pan.
2. Add lean ground beef and cook through.
3. Stir in garlic, ginger, green onion, and basil.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Add soy sauce, fish sauce, honey, and brown sugar.
6. Continue mixing and cooking until the beef is caramelized and slightly crispy.
