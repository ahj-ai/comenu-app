# Chili Crunch Sushi Dip & Sushi Crunch Wrap

A viral, spicy, umami-forward, and crunchy sushi-inspired dip and wrap made with chili crunch.

## Ingredients
- Sushi rice (with rice vinegar, sugar, & mirin)
- Can of tuna
- Mayo
- Momofuku's Chili Crunch
- Circle cut nori
- Batter for round nori (1:1 water to flour ratio)
- Sliced cucumber
- Sliced avocado
- Optional layer (spicy mayo + krab)
- Full sheet of nori

## Steps
1. Prepare sushi rice with vinegar, sugar, and mirin.
2. For the dip: combine tuna, mayo, and chili crunch in a bowl.
3. For the wrap: prepare a round nori by dipping in batter and frying (implied from 'Sushi Crunch Wrap').
4. Layer the wrap with sushi rice, sliced cucumber, sliced avocado, and the tuna dip mixture.
5. Add optional layers like spicy mayo and krab if desired.
6. Wrap everything in a full sheet of nori.
