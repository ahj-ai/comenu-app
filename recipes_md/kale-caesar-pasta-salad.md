# Kale Caesar Pasta Salad

A hearty pasta salad by @grossypelosi featuring baby kale and a homemade Caesar dressing.

**Protein:** None | **Dairy-Free:** Yes | **Prep Time:** 30 mins

## Ingredients
- Salt and black pepper
- 1 pound mezze rigatoni or other short pasta
- 1 cup panko or homemade bread crumbs
- Zest of 2 lemons (reserve lemons for dressing)
- 2 garlic cloves, grated
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 4 cups packed baby kale leaves (5 ounces) or 1-inch Tuscan kale ribbons, stems removed
- 1 1/2 ounces Parmesan shavings (about 1/3 cup)
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan
- 1 (2-ounce) can anchovy fillets, drained (about 12)
- 4 large garlic cloves
- Juice of 2 lemons (1/3 cup)
- 4 teaspoons Dijon mustard

## Steps
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook until al dente. Drain and spread on a tray to cool.
2. In a medium skillet, toast bread crumbs with lemon zest, garlic, 2 tbsp oil, salt, and pepper until golden (4-5 mins).
3. Make the dressing: Blend mayonnaise, grated Parmesan, anchovies, garlic, lemon juice, mustard, salt, and pepper until smooth.
4. In a large bowl, massage kale with remaining 1 tbsp olive oil until tender.
5. Add cooled pasta and 1 1/2 cups of the dressing and toss until well mixed. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
6. Before serving, add Parmesan shavings, half of the breadcrumbs, and extra dressing as needed. Garnish with remaining breadcrumbs.
