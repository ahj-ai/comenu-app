# Creamy Gochujang Beef Udon

A flavorful and creamy udon dish with a spicy gochujang and silken tofu sauce.

**Macros:** 604 Calories | 49gP | 56gC | 18gF

## Ingredients
- 175g Extra Lean Ground Beef (Raw Weight)
- 1 Tbsp Gochujang
- 1 Tsp Sesame Oil
- 2 Tbsp Green Onion
- 1 Tbsp Minced Garlic
- 150g Cooked Udon Noodles
- 225g Silken Tofu
- 1/4 Cup Chicken Broth or Water
- 1 Tsp Stevia
- 2 Tsp Salt
- Black Pepper
- Sesame Seeds

## Steps
1. Into a blender add the silken tofu, 1 Tsp Stevia, 1 Tsp Salt, 1 Tsp Sesame Oil and blend.
2. On a medium high heat, spray with cooking spray and add green onion, minced garlic and 1 Tbsp Gochujang. Cook until fragrant.
3. Add a splash of water or chicken stock and add the ground beef and cook until it has developed a nice golden crust.
4. Cook until most of the liquid has evaporated and the ground beef is fully cooked.
5. Boil udon noodles according to package instructions.
6. Combine the udon noodles, blended silken tofu sauce and ground beef mixture and enjoy!
