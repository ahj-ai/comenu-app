# Mapo Egg Tofu

A high-protein Mapo Tofu variation using eggs and silken tofu.

**Macros:** 40-50g protein

## Ingredients
- 300g of silken tofu
- 2-3 eggs
- 100-150g of minced pork (or chicken/beef)
- Mapo sauce packet
- Green onion
- Chill oil

## Steps
1. Cook minced pork in a pan until cooked. Add mapo sauce and cook for a few more minutes. Set aside.
2. Add a block of silken tofu to a pan and cut into pieces.
3. Whisk eggs and pour onto tofu. Cover to cook for about 3-4 minutes.
4. Add mapo minced pork on top of tofu.
5. Top with green onions and chili oil.
