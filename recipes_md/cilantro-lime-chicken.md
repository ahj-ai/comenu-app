# Cilantro Lime Chicken

A lean and protein-packed chicken recipe.

**Macros:** Calories: 475 kcal, Protein: 45g

## Ingredients
- Chicken
- Cilantro
- Lime

## Steps
1. Marinate chicken with cilantro and lime.
2. Cook until internal temperature reaches safety standards (grilling or pan-searing).
