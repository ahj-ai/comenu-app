# Chili Crunch Sushi Dip Crunchwrap

A viral fusion dish that packs the flavors of sushi dip into a convenient, crispy crunchwrap.

**Protein:** None | **Dairy-Free:** Yes | **Prep Time:** 20 mins

## Ingredients
- Sushi-grade fish or cooked seafood
- Rice
- Chili crunch sauce
- Tortilla wrap

## Steps
1. Mix sushi ingredients together to form a dip.
2. Place the mixture inside a tortilla and fold into a crunchwrap shape.
3. Sear in a pan until the tortilla is golden and crispy.
