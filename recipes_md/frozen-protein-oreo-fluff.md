# Frozen Protein Oreo Fluff

A high-protein, low-calorie frozen dessert that tastes like an Oreo Blizzard.

**Macros:** High protein, low calorie

## Ingredients
- 1 tub sugar-free Cool Whip
- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
- 1 tbsp sugar-free vanilla pudding mix
- 5 Oreos

## Steps
1. Scrape the cream out of the middle of 5 Oreos.
2. Place the chocolate cookies in a bag and smash them into crumbs.
3. Add the vanilla protein powder and sugar-free vanilla pudding mix to the tub of Cool Whip.
4. Add the Oreo crumbs to the mixture.
5. Gently fold the ingredients together slowly to keep the air in the Cool Whip.
6. Freeze for about 3 hours until perfectly frozen.
