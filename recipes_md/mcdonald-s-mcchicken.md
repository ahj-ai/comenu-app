# McDonald's McChicken

A home-made, lower-calorie version of the classic McDonald's McChicken sandwich.

**Protein:** Chicken | **Dairy-Free:** Yes | **Prep Time:** 30 mins

## Tags
- **Culinary Structure:** Bowl / Multi-Component
- **Macro Profile:** Lean Protein Focus
- **Primary Stores:** Safeway

## Normalized Macros
- **Calories:** 196.0

## Ingredients
- Chicken breast
- Seasonings (salt, pepper, etc.)
- Cornflakes
- Romaine lettuce
- Low-calorie hamburger bun
- Low-fat mayo

## Steps
1. Mince the chicken breast and combine with seasonings.
2. Form into patties of approximately 105g each.
3. Coat each patty in crushed cornflakes.
4. Air fry the patties, adding black pepper on top.
5. Assemble the sandwich with a low-calorie bun, low-fat mayo, air-fried chicken patty, and romaine lettuce.
