# Indian Style Eggs (Bhurji)

An incredible and flavorful Indian scrambled egg dish made in a single pan with aromatic spices.

**Macros:** Not specified

## Ingredients
- Eggs
- Ghee
- Red onion
- Cumin seeds
- Green chili
- Ginger garlic paste
- Garam masala
- Turmeric
- Kashmiri red chili powder
- Tomato
- Cilantro
- Salt
- Cheese (optional)

## Steps
1. Melt ghee in a pan and caramelize the red onion.
2. Add cumin seeds and toast until fragrant.
3. Add green chili and ginger garlic paste.
4. Stir in garam masala, turmeric, Kashmiri red chili powder, and salt.
5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and melt into the base.
6. Whisk the eggs and gently fold them into the mixture, cooking until soft.
7. Garnish with fresh cilantro and optional cheese.
