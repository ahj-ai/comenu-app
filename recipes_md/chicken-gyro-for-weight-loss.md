# Chicken Gyro for weight loss

A macro-friendly, low-calorie version of a classic chicken gyro with homemade tzatziki.

**Protein:** Chicken | **Dairy-Free:** No | **Prep Time:** 30 mins

## Ingredients
- Low-fat yogurt
- Lemon juice
- Garlic
- Oregano
- Sazon
- Coriander
- Cumin
- Paprika
- Pepper
- Boneless skinless chicken thighs
- Cucumber
- Dill
- Salt
- Toasted pita
- Red onions
- Tomatoes
- Avocado oil

## Steps
1. Mix yogurt, lemon juice, and mashed garlic for the marinade.
2. Add oregano, sazon, coriander, cumin, paprika, and pepper.
3. Marinate chicken thighs and bake at 375°F until 160°F internal.
4. Grate cucumber, add yogurt, garlic, lemon zest/juice, dill, and salt for tzatziki.
5. Slice chicken into strips and sear in a hot cast iron skillet.
6. Assemble on toasted pita with tzatziki, onions, chicken, and tomatoes.
7. Toast the assembled gyro in a bit of avocado oil.
