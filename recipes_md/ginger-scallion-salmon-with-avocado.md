# Ginger Scallion Salmon with Avocado

A luxury one-pot meal featuring steamed salmon over rice, topped with a bright, raw ginger-scallion sauce, creamy avocado, and a spicy kick.

**Macros:** Not specified

## Ingredients
- Salmon
- Rice
- Ginger
- Scallions
- Avocado (half)
- Spicy sauce
- Sesame seeds

## Steps
1. Prepare a ginger-scallion sauce using raw ingredients to keep it bright and punchy.
2. Steam the salmon directly on top of the rice in one pot.
3. Add half a fresh avocado to the dish.
4. Top with spicy sauce and sesame seeds before serving.
