# Creamy Udon with Silken Tofu

A dairy-free, high-protein udon dish that uses silken tofu to achieve a rich and creamy texture.

**Macros:** Protein: 49g

## Ingredients
- Udon noodles
- Silken tofu
- Sesame oil
- Sweetener

## Steps
1. Blend silken tofu with sesame oil and a touch of sweetener until smooth.
2. Mix the creamy tofu sauce with cooked udon noodles.
