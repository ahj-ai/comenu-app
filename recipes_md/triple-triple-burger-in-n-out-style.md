# Triple Triple Burger (In-N-Out Style)

A macro-friendly version of a massive triple-patty burger with a custom lower-calorie sauce.

**Macros:** 435 Calories | 50.6g Protein | 20g Fat | 22g Carbs

## Ingredients
- 200g 93/7 lean beef (three 70g patties)
- Garlic powder, Onion powder, Black pepper, Salt
- Meat tenderizer
- Mustard (for searing)
- 3 slices American cheese
- Lettuce & Tomato
- Keto Burger bun
- Sauce: 15g light mayo, 8g sweet relish, 8g mustard, 8g ketchup, 1 tsp vinegar, 1 tsp paprika, chopped onions

## Steps
1. Season the lean beef with garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, salt, and meat tenderizer.
2. Form into three thin patties.
3. Sear the patties in a pan, spreading mustard on one side while cooking.
4. Place a slice of American cheese on each patty to melt.
5. Prepare the sauce by mixing all sauce ingredients together.
6. Assemble the burger on a keto bun with lettuce, tomato, the three patties, and the special sauce.
