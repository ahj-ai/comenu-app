# Grilled Asparagus

Simple grilled asparagus seasoned with olive oil, salt, and pepper.

**Macros:** Not specified

## Ingredients
- 1 bundle of asparagus, ends trimmed off
- 1/2 Tbsp avocado oil (or olive oil)
- Salt, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste
- 2 large sweet potatoes, cut into cubes
- 1 Tbsp avocado oil (or olive oil)

## Steps
1. Toss the asparagus with oil and generously season with salt & pepper.
2. Heat your grill (or grill pan) to medium-high heat. Brush with oil.
3. Add the asparagus to the grill and cook for 3-5 minutes or until tender & slightly charred.
4. Toss the potatoes in avocado oil. Generously season with salt & black pepper.
5. Air fry at 390 degrees for 20 minutes, flipping halfway through.
