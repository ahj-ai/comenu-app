# Thai Basil Beef with Chili Crunch Cucumbers

A flavorful and aromatic Thai-inspired ground beef stir fry served with spicy, honey-glazed cucumbers.

**Protein:** Beef | **Dairy-Free:** Yes | **Prep Time:** 30 mins

## Ingredients
- Thai Basil Beef: 2 tbsp beef tallow, 1 yellow onion, red and green bell peppers, 1 lb lean ground beef (90/10), 3-4 tbsp minced garlic, 2 tbsp ginger paste, 3/4 cup green onions, 1.5 cups fresh basil, 1/4 cup soy sauce, 2 tbsp fish sauce, 1/4 cup honey, 2-3 tbsp brown sugar
- Chili Crunch Cucumbers: 1 english cucumber, 2 tbsp chili crunch, 3-4 tbsp honey, 2-3 tbsp seasoned rice vinegar, 2 tbsp sesame seeds, 1 tsp toasted sesame oil, salt and garlic powder
- Toppings: Sriracha mayo, fresh cilantro, sesame seeds

## Steps
1. For cucumbers: Combine all ingredients and toss. Garnish with peanuts and green onions.
2. For beef: Heat beef tallow in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Sauté peppers and onions for a few minutes.
3. Add ground beef and sear to build a crust, then break up and mix with veggies.
4. Add garlic, ginger, green onions, and basil.
5. Season with salt and pepper, then add soy sauce, fish sauce, honey, and brown sugar.
6. Cook until everything is caramelized and crispy.
7. Assemble your bowl with the beef and cucumber salad.
