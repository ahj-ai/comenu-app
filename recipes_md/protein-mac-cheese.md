# Protein Mac & Cheese

A 5-ingredient, high-protein comfort food recipe that takes less than 10 minutes to make.

**Macros:** 375 Calories | 42g Protein | 38g Carbs | 5g Fat

## Ingredients
- 56g Banza pasta (or any chickpea pasta)
- 1 cup bone broth
- 1/2 cup low fat cottage cheese
- 1/2 tbsp nutritional yeast
- 28g light mozzarella cheese

## Steps
1. Cook the pasta in the bone broth in a pan.
2. Blend together the cottage cheese, nutritional yeast, 1 tbsp of water, and any other seasonings you want like garlic, salt, onion, pepper, etc.
3. Once the pasta is cooked, add in the blended mixture and melt the shredded cheese on top.
