# Honey Soy Chicken Salad

Episode 10 of 50-30 Local, featuring a high-protein chicken salad with a honey soy dressing.

**Macros:** Protein: 50g, Time: <30 mins

## Ingredients
- Chicken
- Honey
- Soy sauce
- Salad greens
- 350g Chicken Mince
- 1 tbsp Honey
- 1 tbsp Dark Soy Sauce
- 2 Garlic Cloves
- 2 tsp Avocado Oil
- 1 tbsp Shaoxing Wine or Chicken Stock
- Salt
- 1 Cucumber
- 1/2 Hispi Cabbage
- 2 Corn Cobs
- 1/2 Iceberg Lettuce
- 100g Cherry Tomatoes
- 20g Thai Basil
- 3 Spring Onions
- 300g Silken Tofu (for dressing)
- 1 tbsp Chilli Crisp
- 1 tbsp Peanut butter
- 1 tbsp White Miso
- 1 Lime
- Tajin (optional)
- Sesame Seeds (optional)
- Crispy Onions (optional)

## Steps
1. Prepare chicken (cook until done).
2. Mix honey and soy sauce to create the dressing.
3. Toss chicken and greens with the dressing.
4. Make the sauce: pulse silken tofu in a blender until smooth. Mix in miso, chili crisp, peanut butter, lime juice, and salt. Set aside.
5. Grill corn until charred, then remove kernels from the cob.
6. Shred the Hispi cabbage and iceberg lettuce. Halve the tomatoes, slice spring onions, and julienne the cucumber.
7. Cook chicken in oil until browned. Add honey, dark soy, and grated garlic, cooking for another couple of minutes. Deglaze with wine and season with salt.
8. Combine all vegetables in a bowl, cover with dressing, and mix well.
9. Divide between bowls, top with the chicken, and finish with tajin, sesame seeds, and crispy onions.
