# Air Fryer Hot Honey Chicken

Crispy air-fried chicken bites finished with a sweet and spicy hot honey glaze.

**Macros:** High protein

## Ingredients
- Chicken breast
- Flour
- Eggs
- Panko breadcrumbs
- Honey
- Hot sauce
- Chili flakes

## Steps
1. Dredge chicken pieces in flour, dip in egg wash, and coat with panko.
2. Air fry until crispy and golden.
3. Heat honey with hot sauce and chili flakes for the glaze.
4. Toss the hot chicken in the glaze until well coated.
