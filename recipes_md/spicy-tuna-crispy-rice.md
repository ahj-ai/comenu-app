# Spicy Tuna Crispy Rice

Crunchy fried rice blocks topped with fresh spicy tuna and creamy avocado.

**Protein:** Tuna | **Dairy-Free:** Yes | **Prep Time:** 20 mins

## Ingredients
- Sushi rice
- Rice vinegar
- Sugar
- Sushi-grade tuna
- Sriracha
- Mayo
- Avocado
- Jalapeño
- Oil for frying

## Steps
1. Cook and season sushi rice, then press into a block and chill.
2. Cut into rectangles and fry until golden and crispy.
3. Mix tuna with Sriracha and mayo.
4. Top rice with avocado, spicy tuna, and a slice of jalapeño.
