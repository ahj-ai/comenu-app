# Masala Egg Curry (Dhaba Style)

An easy-to-follow recipe for a flavorful, restaurant-style Indian egg curry.

**Protein:** Egg | **Dairy-Free:** Yes | **Prep Time:** 30 mins

## Ingredients
- 6-7 eggs
- 1 large red onion
- 3-4 tomatoes
- Oil
- Turmeric
- Chili powder
- Salt
- Cinnamon
- Black cardamom
- Green cardamom
- Bay leaf
- Green chilies
- Ginger-garlic paste
- 1.5 cups water
- Fresh cilantro

## Steps
1. Medium boil 6 to 7 eggs so the yolks remain jammy.
2. Heat oil in a pan and fry the eggs for 2-3 minutes with turmeric, chili powder, and salt; then set aside.
3. In the same pan, heat more oil and add whole spices (cinnamon, black cardamom, green cardamom, bay leaf).
4. Add diced onion and cook on medium heat for 8-10 minutes until soft.
5. Add diced tomatoes, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, and remaining spices.
6. Pour in 1.5 cups of water, cover, and cook for 15-20 minutes on medium heat to form the gravy.
7. Finish by adding chopped cilantro and the fried eggs.
