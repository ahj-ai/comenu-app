# Cilantro Lime Chicken Crispy Sandwich

A crispy air-fried chicken sandwich featuring a sweet and tangy cilantro lime sauce and a fresh cucumber salad.

**Macros:** Total: 45g Protein. Chicken: 184 cals/40g P. Sauce: 120 cals. Bread: 115 cals/5g P

## Ingredients
- Chicken breast
- Flour, egg, corn flakes (for dredging)
- Cilantro lime sauce: 1 tbsp avocado oil, 1/2 cup lime juice, 1 tbsp honey, 1 tsp cumin, salt, pepper, 3 garlic cloves, 1/2 cup water
- Ciabatta bread
- Cucumber salad: Cucumber, cabbage, jalapeño, honey pickle juice, salt, pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, lime zest

## Steps
1. Dredge the chicken in flour, then egg, then crushed corn flakes.
2. Air fry at 285F for 18-20 minutes, flipping halfway, until internal temperature reaches 165F.
3. In a pan, combine cilantro lime sauce ingredients and reduce over medium heat.
4. Prepare cucumber salad by slicing vegetables and shaking with the other ingredients.
5. Assemble the sandwich on ciabatta bread with the crispy chicken, reduced sauce, and cucumber salad.
