# Prawn and Citrus Noodle Salad

Episode 11 of 50-30 Local, featuring a high-protein, low-calorie noodle salad with prawns and citrus.

**Macros:** Protein: >50g, Time: <30 mins

## Ingredients
- Prawns
- Citrus (Lemon/Lime juice)
- Noodles
- Vegetables (implied for salad)
- 1 Grapefruit
- 1 Blood Orange
- 1 Cucumber
- 200g cherry Tomatoes
- 3 Spring Onions
- Handful of Mint Leaves
- Handful of Coriander
- 90g Rice noodles, dry weight
- 440g Prawns
- 1 tbsp fish sauce (for prawns)
- White pepper
- 1 Garlic Clove (for prawns)
- 1 Lemon grass Stalk (for prawns)
- 2 tsp Chilli flakes (for prawns)
- 2 tsp Avocado Oil
- 3 tbsp Fish Sauce (for dressing)
- 2 Limes Juice
- 1 tbsp Chilli/garlic paste (optional)
- 1 Lemongrass Stalk (for dressing)
- 1 tbsp Sugar
- 1 Garlic Clove (for dressing)
- 2 Birds Eye chillies

## Steps
1. Prepare noodles according to package instructions.
2. Sauté or grill prawns until cooked.
3. Combine prawns, noodles, and salad ingredients.
4. Dress with citrus-based sauce.
5. Prep the veg: finely chop lemongrass, pick herbs, julienne spring onions and cucumbers, halve cherry tomatoes, and segment citrus fruit.
6. Prepare prawns: pulse prawns in a food processor with half the lemongrass, 1 tbsp fish sauce, a clove of grated garlic, and 2 tsp of chili flakes until minced.
7. Cook prawns in avocado oil for 4-5 minutes, breaking up as you go. Set aside.
8. Make the dressing: pound remaining lemongrass, garlic, and chili into a rough paste. Add remaining dressing ingredients and mix well.
9. Cook noodles in boiling salted water for 2 minutes, drain, and rinse under cold water.
10. Combine noodles, veggies, and prawns in a bowl. Pour over the dressing, mix, and enjoy.
