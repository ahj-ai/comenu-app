# Mapo Tofu

A simple, protein-packed version of the classic Chinese dish Mapo Tofu.

**Macros:** High protein

## Ingredients
- Ground beef
- Garlic
- Ginger
- Green onion
- Cooking wine
- Mapo Tofu sauces (e.g., doubanjiang)
- Cornstarch slurry
- Tofu

## Steps
1. Brown the ground beef in a pan.
2. Add chopped garlic, ginger, and green onion.
3. Pour in a splash of cooking wine.
4. Add the Mapo Tofu sauces.
5. Add a cornstarch slurry to thicken the sauce.
6. Add the tofu at the end and gently mix everything together.
7. Let it simmer before serving.
