# Deli Container Snack (Tuna & Cucumber)

A high-volume, low-calorie savory snack from the Deli Container Snack Series (DCSS).

**Macros:** 222 calories | 31g P | 16g C | 4g F

## Ingredients
- 1 whole cucumber
- 2 tbsp (60g) Greek yogurt
- 10ml lemon juice
- 10g American mustard
- 1/3 red onion
- 1 tin tuna, drained
- 50g sweetcorn, drained
- Salt, pepper, paprika & garlic powder

## Steps
1. Slice the cucumber and red onion thinly using a mandolin.
2. Combine all of the ingredients in a deli container.
3. Put a lid on, shake it all up, and enjoy.
