# Spicy Tofu and Chicken Noodles

A quick and spicy noodle dish featuring both tofu and chicken for high protein content.

**Protein:** Chicken | **Dairy-Free:** Yes | **Prep Time:** 15 mins

## Tags
- **Culinary Structure:** Meal Prep / Batch, Quick / Under 20 Mins
- **Macro Profile:** Lean Protein Focus
- **Primary Stores:** Sprouts, Safeway

## Ingredients
- Chicken breast
- Tofu
- Noodles
- Bok choy
- Soy sauce
- Chili crisp
- Garlic
- Ginger
- Chicken
- Spices
- 300g Chicken Mince
- 2 Garlic Cloves, grated
- 1 inch Ginger, grated
- 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
- 1 tbsp Oyster Sauce
- 2 tsp Mirin
- 330g Silken Tofu
- 1 tbsp Tahini
- 1 tbsp Miso
- 2 tsp Rice Vinegar
- 1 tbsp Gochugaru
- 1 Cucumber
- 2 Spring Onions
- Coriander
- 350g Frozen Udon

## Steps
1. Saute chicken and tofu until cooked through.
2. Boil noodles and drain.
3. Combine soy sauce, chili crisp, garlic, and ginger for the sauce.
4. Toss everything together with bok choy until wilted.
5. Cook tofu and chicken with a blend of spices.
6. Toss with noodles and serve.
7. Make the sauce: pulse silken tofu, tahini, soy sauce, miso, gochugaru, and vinegar in a blender until smooth. Set aside.
8. Julienne the spring onions and pick the coriander. Slice the cucumber into thin rounds and then strips.
9. Cook the chicken in a hot pan until colored. Deglaze with mirin, add soy and oyster sauce, and cook for a few more minutes.
10. Cook the udon noodles according to package instructions.
11. Plate the noodles, pour over the sauce, and top with chicken on one side and the cucumber/herb salad on the other. Finish with sesame seeds.
