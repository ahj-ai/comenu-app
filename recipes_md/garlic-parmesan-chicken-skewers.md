# Garlic Parmesan Chicken Skewers

Savory grilled chicken skewers glazed with a rich garlic parmesan butter.

**Protein:** Chicken | **Dairy-Free:** No | **Prep Time:** 20 mins

## Ingredients
- Chicken breast
- Garlic
- Parmesan cheese
- Butter
- Parsley
- Salt
- Pepper
- Olive oil

## Steps
1. Cube chicken and season with salt, pepper, and olive oil.
2. Thread onto skewers and grill or air fry.
3. Whisk together melted butter, garlic, parmesan, and parsley.
4. Brush the hot chicken skewers with the garlic parmesan mixture.
