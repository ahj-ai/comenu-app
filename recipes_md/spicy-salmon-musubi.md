# Spicy Salmon Musubi

A pocket-sized version of a classic spicy salmon bowl with masago and torched mayo from the Season 3 Musubi series.

**Protein:** Salmon | **Dairy-Free:** Yes | **Prep Time:** 30 mins

## Ingredients
- Salmon
- Rice
- Nori
- Spicy sauce/seasoning
- 2 salmon filets
- Salt
- Pepper
- 1/4 cup Kewpie mayo
- Half a lemon/lime
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp masago
- 1/2 tbsp sriracha
- 3 cups rice
- Nori sheets
- Furikake
- Musubi mold
- Torch

## Steps
1. Season the salmon with a spicy mixture.
2. Assemble the musubi by layering rice and salmon, then wrapping with nori.
3. Wash rice thoroughly and cook it.
4. Slice salmon into musubi-shaped sizes and season lightly with salt and pepper.
5. Cook salmon on low-medium heat until all sides are brown. Set aside.
6. Prepare sauce by mixing kewpie mayo, lemon/lime, sugar, sriracha, and masago.
7. Cut 4 nori sheets into halves.
8. Assemble musubi by filling 2/3 of your musubi mold with cooked rice. Press down with mold. Sprinkle furikake.
9. Press mold down all the way then release the outside mold while pressing down.
10. Layer your mayo sauce, top with cooked salmon, then spread more spicy mayo sauce on top.
11. Torch the mayo until slightly charred.
12. Wrap with nori sheet with 'shiny' side outside and 'rough' side inside.
