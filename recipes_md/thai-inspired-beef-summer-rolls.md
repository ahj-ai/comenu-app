# Thai Inspired Beef Summer Rolls

Refreshing summer rolls filled with seasoned extra lean ground beef and served with a high-protein peanut dipping sauce.

**Macros:** High protein

## Ingredients
- 2 lbs extra lean ground beef
- 1/2 cup chopped green onions
- 1/2 cup shredded carrots
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 spoonful chopped garlic
- Onion powder
- 1 tbsp lemongrass paste
- 1 tbsp basil paste
- Spring roll wrappers
- Jalapenos
- Cilantro
- Mixed greens
- PB2 powder (for sauce)
- Fish sauce (for sauce)
- Thai red curry paste (for sauce)
- Honey (for sauce)
- 2 lb(s) Extra Lean Ground Beef
- 9 piece Spring Roll Wrappers
- 1/2 cup grated carrots
- 1 tbsp garlic
- 2 cups mixed baby greens
- 1 tbsp onion powder
- 4 green onions
- 1/3 cup cilantro
- 1 fresh jalapeño
- Peanut Sauce: 4 tbsp PB2 powder, 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1/4 cup water, 1 tbsp garlic, 1 tbsp Thai red chili paste, 1 tbsp fish oil, 1 tbsp honey

## Steps
1. Cook the extra lean ground beef with green onions, shredded carrots, soy sauce, garlic, onion powder, lemongrass paste, and basil paste.
2. Dip a spring roll wrapper quickly in water and place on a cutting board.
3. Layer the wrapper with jalapenos, 1/2 cup of the beef mixture, green onions, cilantro, and mixed greens.
4. Fold the top and bottom of the wrapper over the filling, then roll it up like a burrito.
5. Prepare the dipping sauce by mixing PB2, soy sauce, fish sauce, garlic, water, red curry paste, and honey until smooth.
6. Cook the ground beef with garlic, lemongrass paste, basil paste, onion powder, and soy sauce until browned.
7. Prepare the peanut sauce by mixing PB2, soy sauce, water, garlic, chili paste, fish oil, and honey.
8. Dip a spring roll wrapper in water to soften.
9. Fill with cooked beef, grated carrots, green onions, cilantro, jalapeño, and baby greens.
10. Roll up tightly and serve with the peanut dipping sauce.
