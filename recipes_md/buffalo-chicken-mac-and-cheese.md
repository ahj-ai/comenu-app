# Buffalo Chicken Mac and Cheese

A spicy, cheesy twist on classic mac and cheese with buffalo-seasoned chicken.

**Protein:** Chicken | **Dairy-Free:** No | **Prep Time:** 30 mins

## Ingredients
- Macaroni pasta
- Shredded chicken
- Buffalo sauce
- Cheddar cheese
- Mozzarella
- Cream cheese
- Ranch dressing
- Green onions

## Steps
1. Cook pasta and set aside.
2. Toss shredded chicken in buffalo sauce.
3. Melt cream cheese with cheddar, mozzarella, and ranch to make the sauce.
4. Combine pasta, chicken, and sauce in a baking dish.
5. Top with remaining cheese and bake until bubbly.
6. Garnish with sliced green onions.
