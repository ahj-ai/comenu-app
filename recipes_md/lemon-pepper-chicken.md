# Lemon Pepper Chicken

An easy, flavorful chicken dish ready in 30 minutes with a fresh avocado-feta topping.

**Macros:** Time: ~30 mins

## Ingredients
- Chicken
- Lemon juice
- Lemon zest
- Paprika
- Garlic
- Avocado
- Feta cheese
- Fresh dill
- Chives
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Steps
1. Marinate chicken in lemon juice, lemon zest, paprika, and garlic.
2. Roast chicken until perfectly tender and juicy.
3. Prepare topping by tossing avocado, feta, dill, and chives with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
4. Spoon the avocado-feta topping over the chicken and serve with an extra squeeze of lemon.
