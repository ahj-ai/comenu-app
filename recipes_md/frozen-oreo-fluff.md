# Frozen Oreo Fluff

A high-protein frozen dessert treat featuring Oreo crumbs and protein powder.

**Protein:** None | **Dairy-Free:** No | **Prep Time:** 20 mins

## Tags
- **Culinary Structure:** Quick / Under 20 Mins
- **Macro Profile:** Snack / Sweet Tooth
- **Primary Stores:** Target

## Normalized Macros
- **Calories:** 190.0
- **Protein:** 8.0g
- **Carbs:** N/A
- **Fat:** N/A

## Ingredients
- 1 tub zero sugar cool whip
- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
- 1 tbsp sugar-free pudding mix
- 5 Oreo cookies without cream

## Steps
1. Gently fold in your protein powder, sf pudding mix, and oreo chocolate cookie crumbs (no cream) into your sugar free cool whip.
2. Freeze up to 3 hours & enjoy your frozen treat.
